Mazor-Steiner is an Israeli boutique law firm specializing in torts, insurance law and professional malpractice, including physicians, attorneys and engineers. Since 1950 we have been providing outstanding legal representation to both private clients and leading insurance companies, situating ourselves as one of the leading law firms in its field.

Mazor-Steiner Law firm was founded in 1950 by the late Attorney Uri MAzor, a graduate of one of the first classes of Hebrew University Law school. Attorney Mazor took part in the establishment of Israeli insurance companies, adapting their policies to the local needs and circumstances. Over the years Attorney Mazor trained many students who interned in the firm and later on continued to succeed on their own. The excelling interns remained in the firm, and continue his legacy, after his death in 2008.

The firm has sixty years of experience in the fields of torts and insurance law, and was involved in the amendment of insurance policies in reaction to the legislation of laws, such as the Insurance Contract Law, the Liability for Damaged Products Law and various other consumer protection laws. In addition, the firm drafted insurance policies for legal malpractice.

The firm has a reputation of representing free professionals, and mainly lawyers, doctors, engineers, architects, accountants, insurance agents and appraisers. Throughout the firm’s sixty years of practice its work has contributed to the creation of many important precedents in the fields of torts and insurance, including the issue of double insurance coverage, statute of limitations in insurance and torts claims, compensation for wrongful birth, and the definition of the standard of care required in various professions, such as lawyers, doctors, engineers and insurance agents.

Approximately sixty percent of the firm’s workload is devoted to the representation of defendants, and the remainder to the representation of plaintiffs in personal damage claims as a result of car accidents, work accidents, medical malpractice and damage to property. As part of its services the firm provides representation in procedures involving the National Insurance Institute. In the last few years, due to demand on the part of recurring clients, the firm has begun providing legal services in the areas of real estate and expropriation. In addition, the firm regularly provides pro bono legal services.

Over the years the firm has zealously maintained itself as a boutique law firm, allowing it to provide its clients with professional, personal and efficient services by highly skilled and experienced lawyers.
