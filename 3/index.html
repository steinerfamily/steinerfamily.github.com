Donate Without Spending a Penny

AdDonate is an add-on to your browser, which cusomize your every-day surfing and transforms it into an act of donation.

AdDonate translates your online exposure to ads into donations to an organization of your choice.

All this is accomplished without comprimising your web experience or infringing upon your privacy.
